Context

"Carnal" is an innovative art collaboration between Mexico's Secretary of Culture and French artists. This captivating installation focuses on intimate interviews with Mexican locals, capturing the unique stories of their lives through vivid hand photographs. This cross-cultural endeavor seamlessly intertwines perspectives, celebrating the beauty of human connection while bridging the gap between nations.

Results (metrics).

The project culminated with a successful dual-site launch, accompanied by the seamless handover of server and cloud service access credentials to the client. To ensure a smooth transition, we meticulously crafted comprehensive manuals and guides. These resources empowered the client with clear instructions, safeguarding against confusion and equipping them to confidently manage and navigate the installed systems, upholding the project's triumphant conclusion.
