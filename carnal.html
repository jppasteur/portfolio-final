Carnal

"Carnal" is an innovative art collaboration between Mexico's Secretary of Culture and French artists. This captivating installation focuses on intimate interviews with Mexican locals, capturing the unique stories of their lives through vivid hand photographs. This cross-cultural endeavor seamlessly intertwines perspectives, celebrating the beauty of human connection while bridging the gap between nations.

I created complete digital identity creation for two websites, the installation interface, and an internal database system. My role encompassed crafting cohesive visual elements, optimizing user experience, and ensuring seamless data management. This holistic approach resulted in unified branding, user-friendly interaction, and efficient information handling across all facets of the project.

The installation featured a captivating 3D interactive menu, offering the freedom to traverse amidst a myriad of stars in any orientation. Upon selection, each star unveiled an interview accompanied by a poignant photograph and compelling audio, creating an immersive narrative cosmos. This innovative blend of technology and storytelling engendered a profound and multisensory visitor experience.

The implementation of the 3D menu was made possible by leveraging the capabilities of three.js. This choice stemmed from the project's artistic essence and the substantial volume of content, surpassing 1200 interviews, as envisioned by the client. three.js offered a versatile platform to seamlessly merge artistic expression with technological prowess, resulting in a dynamic and visually captivating user interface.

The database design adopted an elegant and minimalistic approach, tailored exclusively for the client's use, distinct from user interactions. By prioritizing simplicity, we aimed to prevent any potential client confusion regarding diverse interactions. This strategy ensured an intuitive and focused interface, enhancing efficiency and facilitating seamless management of the vast interview and multimedia assets within the installation.

To enhance the utility of the internal database, we introduced essential functionalities. The project culminated with a successful dual-site launch, accompanied by the seamless handover of server and cloud service access credentials to the client. To ensure a smooth transition, we meticulously crafted comprehensive manuals and guides. These resources empowered the client with clear instructions, safeguarding against confusion and equipping them to confidently manage and navigate the installed systems, upholding the project's triumphant conclusion.

The venture achieved resounding success, fostering an enduring collaboration that persists to the present day.
