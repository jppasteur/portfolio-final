My Work

As a web-oriented designer, I have been able to develop and polish a different set of tools that have guided me through my projects, helping me achieve new grounds.

Throughout my career, I have developed robust design systems that encompass all the UI specifications of a website, from concept to code implementation.

I am constantly learning and expanding my abilities, with a particular proficiency in creating design systems, UI kits, and brand identities.

As a 3D artist, I boast a diverse background, ranging from highly creative projects like music videos to business-oriented websites that incorporate 3D elements.

By staying at the vanguard of innovation, we can provide a technological edge that many older websites lack. This differentiation can transform a last-generation website into a highly modernized one.

I have achieved significant success with modern technologies, including interactive three.js elements, designing AR and VR experiences, as well as exploring photogrammetry and 3D printing.
