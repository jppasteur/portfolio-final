Context

Nulla Dies is an intriguing online photographic installation. Utilizing cutting-edge AI algorithms integrated into the platform, it can craft brief videos from user prompts. This project was commissioned by the Mexican Secretary of Culture. My role encompassed IT research and management, interface design, user research, and user experience enhancement, ensuring a seamless blend of technology and artistry.

Results & Metrics

With an incredibly tight deadline of just one month, I had to make the most of our resources. I brought in a full-stack developer, and together, we not only managed to complete the website but also developed an internal database for the client's information management. As a bonus, we crafted a comprehensive UI Kit document, which we promptly handed over to the client, showcasing our efficiency and productivity.
