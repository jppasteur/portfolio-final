WEWE Global, a marketing agency with a niche focus on finance and cryptocurrency, sought my expertise to create an engaging landing page. They aimed to expand their target audience. In this project, I assumed the role of a No-Code Developer, taking charge of the entire design process. Leveraging the built-in tools provided by WordPress, I single-handedly executed all aspects of the project, ensuring its success relied solely on my skills and expertise.
